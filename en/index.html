About Me

I am a Brazilian researcher and AI Tech Lead at Anota AI, where I lead innovations in intelligent chatbot systems and data-driven services. I hold a Master's degree in Computer Engineering from FURG, with research focused on mathematical functions applied to Transformer architectures to enhance interpretability. My work bridges practical industry solutions with theoretical advances in neuro-symbolic AI, fuzzy logic, and natural language processing.
